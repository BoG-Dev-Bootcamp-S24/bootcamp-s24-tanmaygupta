So What is F1


Formula 1 (F1) is the premier motorsport featuring top drivers racing high-performance cars on circuits worldwide. Known for its advanced technology, strategic pit stops, and intense competition, F1 culminates in a World Champion crowned at the end of each season. With its global audience and blend of speed and skill, F1 is a thrilling spectacle.

The Journey to F1


Aspiring F1 drivers typically start in karting or junior racing series, then progress through Formula 4, Formula 3, and Formula 2, gaining experience and catching the attention of F1 teams. Successful racers may become test or reserve drivers before securing a full-time seat in Formula 1, requiring a lot of talent, determination, and financial support.
